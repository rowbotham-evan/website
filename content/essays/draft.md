Errantic. Unpredictable. Genius. All words we commonly use to describe the visionaries: Steve Jobs, Kanye West, Stephen Curry,.. In my first seven months being in NYC and being plugged into Silicion Valley via X, I have realized that they both share a permeating narrative in their respective cultures: "I'm the main character surrounded by (at best) NPCs." 


There is an issue with that particular statement. But this statement is illustrative of a larger cultural problem that is prevalent in the NBA, Universities, and Careers. Everyone wants to be *the* main character and will not even settle for *a* main character.
