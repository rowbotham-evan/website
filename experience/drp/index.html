Optimal way to play Blackjack


Independendy Study, DRP
Jan 2023 – May 2023

This was an independent study conducted under Farez Siddiqui, through the Math Department’s Directed Reading Program (DRP).

  • Researched reinforment learning with an emphasis on applications in finance. My primary research text was Barto and Sutton’s Reinforcement Learning.
  • Designed an RL model using monte carlo simulations to find the optimal policy for playing blackjack. You can find it on my github, here.
  • Presented my project at FSU’s Undergraduate Research Symposium.
