Despite math’s “unreasonable effectiveness”, it never provided me with what writing does, clarity. I enjoy writing about my reflections on life, discussing limiting belief systems I am aspiring to overcome, and how to hopefully impact the world.  My main goal for these essays, however, is the same I have in all my conversations: I hope to make you feel just the tiniest bit lighter after it.
