Despite math’s “unreasonable effectiveness”, it never provided me with what writing does, clarity. I enjoy writing about questions I think about for too long, my decision making process, and how to live an anti-fragile life.  My main goal for these essays, however, is the same I have in all my conversations: I hope to make you feel just the tiniest bit lighter after it.
